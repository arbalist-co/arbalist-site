Founding Team

We are a group of passionate Computer Science students from Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science. Brought together by our shared vision of creating innovative technology solutions, we founded Arbalist to push the boundaries of what's possible in software development.

Our diverse backgrounds and complementary skill sets allow us to tackle complex problems with creativity and precision. From web applications to machine learning models, we're committed to building tools that make a difference.
